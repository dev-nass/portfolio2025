# About Me

Hello world! I'm Jonas Vince Macawile, currently a 3rd-year college student at Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Dasmariñas (KLD), taking up a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS).

Since then, I’ve been sitting in front of my laptop every day, continuously learning new things. Because of those efforts, I gradually noticed improvements in my skills. That progress made me passionate if not addicted to coding every day.
